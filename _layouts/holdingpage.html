Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy set out below forms part of these Terms of Use and sets out the way in which your personal data are handled. Therefore please read this Policy carefully, as it explains what information we will collect about you, how we will use it, and the steps we take to ensure that it is kept safe.

What information do we collect?

In order to provide you with our services, we may collect personal information about you from our website, emails, and written and verbal communications. We may, for example, keep a record of your name, address, delivery address, email address, telephone number and credit card details. We may also record details of any disability or health needs you may have at the time of booking an event or service to help to ensure your safety.

This website is in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. When you visit our site to read or download information, we log your IP address which is automatically recognised by the web server. We do not make this information available to third parties, nor do we store or capture personal information.

Mailing list

There is a mailing list option on the site. The system will record your e-mail address and other information only if you subscribe. If you do sign up for our mailing list your data will not be disclosed to any company outside of Tamarind Collection and we will only contact you with news about Tamarind Collection restaurants. You can remove your details from the mailing list at any time. Your email address will not be passed on to third parties.

Copyrights policy

The Tamarind Collection website includes a variety of materials created by members of Tamarind Collection team. Although these works may be freely accessible on the Internet and may not include any statement about copyright, the works are protected by copyright. Users must assume that works are protected by copyright until they learn otherwise.

Data

We collect data about how our site is searched to enable us to understand and respond to the needs of our users. These data do not contain personally identifiable information. You can visit our site without identifying yourself or disclosing any personal information.
